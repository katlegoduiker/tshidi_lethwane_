A little about me

Tshidi Lethwane is passionate about women, personal development, mental health, and self-love/awareness. She shares her personal experiences, which make her relatable to clients, and her superpower is helping each of her clients become the best version of themselves, the best of the best. She inspires her clients to go after their dreams and live their best life.

Her passion for women and her desire to inspire them led her to take a leap of faith and start Kekaboentle, a Setswana phrase meaning “I am a Beautiful Gift.” This phrase reminds women how precious they are and that they are a gift, regardless of what they face. Another objective was for her to impact, renew, and transform their minds in whatever situation they find themselves in.

This is achieved by hosting regular events with topics ranging from mental health, the relationship with self, beauty, and health. The events provide opportunities for women to regain their throne by dealing with the issues they face and be equipped to deal with things moving forward. She does this by using the skills she acquired as an NLP coach. Her clients range from young women, teens, and older women. It’s exciting to see lives change, and people begin to visualize their ideal future.
